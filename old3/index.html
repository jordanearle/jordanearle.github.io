About Me

I’m an 18 year old student from Belfast, Northern Ireland with a passion for great design. I’ve previously been involved in a number of projects and start-ups including Thinkspace, YouthEast and Create.

Currently, I'm a first year student at the University of Ulster studying Interaction Design.

When I’m not working on projects or university work, I love to watch TV shows or take far too many photos of my dog.

I'm constantly on the lookout for new ideas and projects to get involved with, so do get in touch.
