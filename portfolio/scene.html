Scene is an intelligent television and film tracker for your iPhone, letting you keep track of your favourite films and shows with ease.

Timeline

TV Guide was first published in 1953, since then, millions of Americans have relied on a bi-weekly printed magazine to keep up with their favourite television shows and discover new ones. Scene, takes this concept and brings it into the digital age. With a innovative timeline format, combining television listings and movie release dates into one, easy-to-use timeline.

Scene also lets you browse back in time. Take a walk back through memory lane, checking premiere dates for previous seasons of your favourite shows. Timeline will help you keep track of your favoruite shows and movies like never before.

Design

Scene makes use of a clean and simple to use interface. Relying heavily on typography and imagery, allowing you to focus on the content. Iconography and intuitive gestures allow for easy navigation throughout the app. Design and user experience were a key focus for us during development, ensuring legibility and usability for users.
