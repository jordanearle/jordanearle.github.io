YouthEast was an application I designed and founded with Matthew Carson (who previously was MYP for East Belfast.) It aimed to help local young people find things to do in their area. .



Visual identity - The design for this logo was to be kept fairly simplistic, the colour choice was to ensure that the colour did not have any political connotations, whilst the map icon helps suggest function.
User Interface - Two screenshots from the final app. The app used a traditional tab bar layout, with the user navigating between the different categories available from the bottom. Tapping on the map would reveal a combined map view, showing the nearest activity centre.

Video - A video highlighting the various animations we utilised in the application. With the onset of iOS 7, many applications lacked personality, so we hoped to bring some of this back with interesting animations.
