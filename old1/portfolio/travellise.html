Travellise
BACK TO PORTFOLIO

Travellise is an award winning entry for the Generation Innovation Internship Prize. I was tasked with designing a number of screens to produce an interactive mockup for my client who then pitched the app to a number of judges. Travellise won the pitching competition and received £10,000 in funding.




User Interface - Two screenshots from the final app. The app used a traditional tab bar layout, with the user navigating between the different categories available from the bottom. Tapping on the map would reveal a combined map view, showing the nearest activity centre.
