Create:2014 was a one day creative conference for young people in Derry~Londonderry that took place during CultureTech 2014. Create14 has featured speakers from the BBC, Facebook, Microsoft, Playstation and more.




Visual identity - For the event’s initial year, we wanted a bold and bright identity that would communicate playfulness and appeal to a young crowd. The hexagon communicated the 6 members of the team.
Social Graphic - One out of a series of graphics designed for social media. Some of these were to encourage ticket sales, others to encourage Kickstarter backers and several were just informational.

Speaker Graphic - A series of graphics I created to promote and announce our initial speakers for the event. This is one example of those.
